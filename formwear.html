Form Wear

Let fashion savvy users from around the world pick out your next wardrobe.

A lot of us often times struggle with the daunting task of picking out a good-looking outfit. Some slave away for hours deciding between combinations of clothes while others complacently just throw on the first thing they see. Form Wear simplifies this painful process by allowing other users to recommend outfits best suited for the user's style, personality, and appearance. Users will then have review these suggestions and decide for themselves which selection is bested suited for them.

This was an app I began working on at the Product Hunt Hakcathon 2014. It ended up being one of my first iOS projects where I really wanted to emphasize the app's aesthetic design. Special thanks to Shopstyle for allowing me to use their api.
