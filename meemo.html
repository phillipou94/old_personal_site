Meemo

Your Social Journaling Companion

Organize all your cherished memory in one elegant, easy to use application. Share your favorite stories now and relive them with the people you love. Create groups to share all the moments you and your friends have experienced together. You create the memories you've always wanted to share with the people you care about the most.

This was a very fun design project that let me familiarize myself some more with the new Swift programming language. It allowed me to mock up the design on Sketch and implement the views and animations all from scratch. I also decided to use the MVVM design paradigm with and really appreciated how much it cleaned up the logic in view controllers throughout the app. Really fun project to work on and looking forward to using this with my friends.
