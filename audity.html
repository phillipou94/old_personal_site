Audity

Your Open Sourced Playlist

Audity is a social music recommendation service that allows users to post a status of what they are doing and what music they want to listen to. Other users can then contribute songs to these dynamic playlists. While there are a number of applications that utilize algorithms to conduct music recommendations, I often find myself disregarding their suggestions all together and listening to what my friends recommend.

After all, a recommendation from someone close to you bears much more significance than one generated by an algorithm. There are a number of outlets with the capability to share media, but no one true platform that focuses entirely on that experience. Audity seeks to provide that service to users and create a fun, music-loving community that are just as eager about sharing their songs as listening to them.
